# SmartQueue AI

> **Skip the Queue. Save Hours.**

An AI-powered queue management platform built for Indian government offices — RTOs, hospitals, passport offices, municipal corporations, and Aadhaar centres.

---

## What is SmartQueue AI?

Every day, millions of Indians waste hours standing in queues at government offices. SmartQueue AI fixes that.

Citizens book a digital token from home, track their position live, and walk in only when their turn is close. Government staff manage queues from a centralized dashboard. An AI engine predicts crowd levels in advance so everyone — staff and citizens — can plan better.

## Key Features

- **Digital Token Booking** — Book from anywhere, anytime
- **Live Queue Tracking** — See exactly how many people are ahead
- **AI Crowd Prediction** — Know the best time to visit before you leave home
- **Smart Notifications** — Get alerted when your turn is approaching
- **Walk-in Support** — Reception desk can issue offline tokens too
- **Priority Handling** — Senior citizens and disabled persons served first
- **Public Display Screen** — Large-format token board for office waiting rooms
- **Self-Learning AI** — Model improves automatically every day from real data
- **Multi-language Support** — Hindi, Gujarati, Tamil, Telugu and more

---

## The Three Parts

### 1. Citizen Portal
A clean, mobile-friendly website where citizens book tokens, track queues, and view AI predictions for their nearest government office.

### 2. Government Staff Dashboard
A professional admin panel where staff call the next token, manage counters, generate walk-in tokens, view analytics, and act on AI alerts.

### 3. AI Prediction Engine
A machine learning backend that predicts crowd levels, wait times, and token counts for each office — accounting for Ahmedabad-specific patterns like Navratri, Uttarayan, month-end tax deadlines, and monsoon seasons. The model learns from real data every day and retrains itself every week.

India has over 50,000 government service centres. The average citizen visits at least 3–4 times a year. SmartQueue AI is built to make every one of those visits faster, smarter, and less stressful — for everyone.
